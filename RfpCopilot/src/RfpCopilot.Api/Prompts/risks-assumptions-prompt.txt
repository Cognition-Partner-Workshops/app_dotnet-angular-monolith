## Assumptions

### Technical Assumptions
1. The client's existing infrastructure supports modern cloud-native deployments
2. APIs for third-party integrations are well-documented and accessible
3. Development and testing environments will be provisioned within 2 weeks of project start
4. The client will provide VPN/network access for the development team within 1 week
5. Existing data is in a structured format suitable for migration

### Business Assumptions
6. A dedicated product owner from the client side will be available for the project duration
7. Business requirements will be finalized and signed off within the Discovery phase
8. Change requests after design phase will follow a formal change control process
9. The client will participate in bi-weekly sprint reviews and provide timely feedback
10. Budget approval for cloud infrastructure costs is obtained before the Build phase

### Resource Assumptions
11. Key resources will be available as planned without unexpected attrition
12. Client SMEs will be available for knowledge transfer sessions (minimum 4 hours/week)
13. Third-party vendors will provide support and access within agreed SLAs

### Timeline Assumptions
14. No major regulatory changes will impact the project scope during implementation
15. Holiday schedules and planned leaves are accounted for in the timeline

### Infrastructure Assumptions
16. Cloud accounts and subscriptions will be provisioned before the Design phase
17. SSL certificates and domain names will be provided by the client
18. Production deployment windows will be agreed upon during the Design phase

## Risk Response Strategies
- **Avoid**: Eliminate the risk by changing project approach
- **Mitigate**: Reduce probability or impact through proactive actions
- **Transfer**: Shift risk to third party (insurance, vendor contracts)
- **Accept**: Acknowledge and monitor (for low-impact risks)

## Risk Monitoring Process
1. Weekly risk review in project status meetings
2. Monthly risk register update and scoring
3. Escalation to steering committee for risks scoring 6+
4. Risk closure criteria and documentation
